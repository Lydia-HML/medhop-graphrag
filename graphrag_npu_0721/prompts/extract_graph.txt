-Goal-
Extract a small biomedical knowledge graph from the text. Follow the exact format.

-Rules-
1. Select at most 8 important entities of these types: [{entity_types}]. Prefer IDs such as DB08918, P31645, P56817, genes, proteins, drugs, diseases and pathways.
2. Entity description must be at most 12 words.
3. Emit up to 12 explicit relationships. Every relationship source and target must exactly match an emitted entity name.

5. Output records only. No XML tags, headings, explanations, markdown, or JSON.
6. Separate records with ## and end with <|COMPLETE|>.

Entity format:
("entity"<|>NAME<|>TYPE<|>SHORT DESCRIPTION)

Relationship format:
("relationship"<|>SOURCE<|>TARGET<|>SHORT EVIDENCE<|>STRENGTH)
